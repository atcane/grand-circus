Cloverly, MD

Location

Cloverly, MD is a town located witin Montgomery County. This part of the county is often referred to as the greater Washington, D.C. area.

About

Population

Approximately 15,062 residents live in Clovery.

Geography

Many residents consider themselves to live in the better known neighborhood of Silver Spring as the areas overlap. Cloverly itself has a total area of 3.9 square miles.

Suburb

Cloverly and surrounding cities are considered a suburb of Washington, D.C. and are coveted due to the rural/suburb feel with land and space, yet close proximity to the metropolitan area of the nation's capital.

Things To Do

  • Visit the nation's capital
  • Walk amongst nature
  • Visit a local apple orchard
